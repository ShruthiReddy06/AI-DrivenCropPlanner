Okra (Lady's Finger) Cultivation Guide

1. Land Preparation

  • Plow the field 2-3 times to ensure fine tilth and remove weeds.
  • Apply well-rotted FYM at the time of last plowing.
  • Form ridges and furrows 60 cm apart for sowing.

2. Seed Selection & Sowing

  • Use high-yield, disease-resistant hybrid seeds.
  • Soak seeds overnight to improve germination.
  • Sow seeds at 30 cm apart on ridges, 2–3 cm deep.

3. Water Management

  • Water immediately after sowing and at regular intervals.
  • Maintain soil moisture, especially during flowering and fruiting.
  • Avoid waterlogging to prevent root diseases.

4. Fertilization Schedule

  • Basal dose: 25:50:25 NPK kg/acre before sowing.
  • Top dressing: Apply 25 kg Urea after 30 days of sowing.

5. Weed & Pest Management

  • Weed twice — once after 20 days, then at 40 days.
  • Pests: Aphids, Fruit borers — use neem oil or recommended insecticides.
  • Diseases: Powdery mildew — treat with sulfur-based fungicides.

6. Investment Breakdown (Per Acre)

7. Harvesting & Yield

  • First harvest after 45–50 days of sowing.
  • Harvest tender pods every 2–3 days.
  • Yield: 50–70 quintals per acre.

8. Market Rate & Profit Estimation

  • Market price: ₹2,000 – ₹4,000 per quintal (varies by season).
  • Gross returns: ₹1,00,000 – ₹2,80,000 per acre.
  • Net profit: ₹80,000 – ₹2,00,000 per acre.
