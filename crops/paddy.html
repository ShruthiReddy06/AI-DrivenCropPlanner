Paddy (Rice) Cultivation Guide

1. Land Preparation

  • Plow the field 2-3 times to soften the soil and remove weeds.
  • Flood the field with 3–5 inches of water and plow again to create a muddy surface (puddling).
  • Level the field to ensure uniform water distribution.

2. Seed Selection & Sowing

  • Use high-yield, disease-resistant seed varieties.
  • Soak seeds in 10% saltwater for 10 minutes (discard floating seeds).
  • Pre-germinate seeds by soaking overnight and incubating under moist cloth for 1 day.
  • Transplant 20–30 day-old seedlings into the main field with 20 cm spacing.

3. Water Management

  • Initial Stage: Maintain 2–3 inches of water for the first 3 weeks.
  • Tillering Stage: Keep 4–5 cm water; drain for 2–3 days if needed.
  • Flowering Stage: Reduce watering; keep field moist.
  • Final Drain: Stop irrigation 10–15 days before harvesting.

4. Fertilization Schedule

  • Basal Dose: Urea (30–40 kg), DAP (20–25 kg), MOP (15–20 kg), FYM (5–10 tons).
  • Tillering Stage (20–30 days): Urea (20–25 kg).
  • Flowering Stage (45–60 days): Urea (15–20 kg) and Potash.

5. Weed & Pest Management

  • Weeds: Butachlor (2 kg/acre); manual weeding at 20–25 days.
  • Pests: Stem Borer, Gall Midge — use recommended insecticides.
  • Diseases: Blast & Sheath Blight — use fungicides as per local guidelines.

Seeds ₹600 – ₹1,000

Total Investment ₹20,000 – ₹25,000 per acre

7. Harvesting & Yield

  • Harvest when 80–85% of grains turn golden yellow.
  • Expected yield: 20–30 quintals per acre.

8. Market Rate & Profit Estimation

  • Average market price: ₹2,000 – ₹3,500 per quintal.
  • Gross returns per acre: ₹40,000 – ₹1,05,000.
  • Net profit: ₹15,000 – ₹80,000 per acre.
