Beans Cultivation Guide

1. Land Preparation

  • Beans prefer well-drained loamy soils rich in organic matter.
  • 2–3 ploughings with leveling and FYM (8–10 tons/acre) incorporation.

2. Sowing & Seed Rate

  • Time: October to December or early summer.
  • Seed rate: 8–10 kg/acre.
  • Spacing: 30 cm x 10 cm (row x plant).

3. Water Management

  • Provide light irrigation after sowing and maintain moisture during flowering and pod development.
  • Avoid water stagnation to prevent root diseases.

4. Fertilization Schedule

  • Apply basal dose of FYM + 15:45:30 NPK kg/acre.
  • Top dressing with Urea (10 kg/acre) at 25 days after sowing.

5. Pest & Disease Management

  • Pests: Aphids, pod borers — control with neem spray or safe insecticides.
  • Diseases: Rust, anthracnose — treat with copper fungicides or carbendazim.

7. Harvesting & Yield

  • First harvest is ready in 45–60 days depending on the variety.
  • Expected yield: 40–60 quintals per acre (green pods).

8. Market Rate & Profit

  • Average market rate: ₹2,000 – ₹4,000 per quintal.
  • Gross returns: ₹80,000 – ₹2,40,000 per acre.
  • Net profit: ₹60,000 – ₹2,20,000 per acre.
